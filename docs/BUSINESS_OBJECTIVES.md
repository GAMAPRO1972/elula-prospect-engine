# Business Objectives

## Primary Objective

Generate qualified appointments for Elula Business Dynamics.

Appointments are the primary KPI. Lead volume matters only when it improves the number and quality of qualified appointments.

## Why the Project Exists

Elula Prospect Engine exists to create a repeatable prospecting operation.

The system discovers businesses, processes their data, prepares sales intelligence, prevents duplicates, and imports qualified prospects into Elula BizHub for structured follow-up.

## How It Supports Elula BizHub

Elula BizHub is the central CRM, automation, communication, and pipeline platform.

The Prospect Engine supports Elula BizHub by:

- creating or updating CRM contacts;
- creating opportunities;
- creating initial follow-up tasks;
- routing prospects to owners;
- supporting pipeline management;
- keeping imports controlled and measurable.

## How It Supports Red XRay

Red XRay can be positioned when prospects show public digital footprint, website exposure, or security-relevant online presence.

Website intelligence and future Google Business Profile Intelligence can support Red XRay positioning by identifying digital risk, visibility, and online infrastructure signals.

## How It Supports Elula Skills

Elula Skills can be positioned when prospect context indicates training, onboarding, compliance, workforce development, or operational process needs.

Future AI call preparation should recommend Elula Skills only when the prospect profile supports that conversation.

## How It Supports Elula Compliance

Elula Compliance can be positioned when a prospect operates in a regulated, risk-sensitive, or process-heavy sector.

Future enrichment should identify compliance triggers and prepare relevant discovery questions.

## How It Supports Elula Mobile

Elula Mobile can be positioned when mobile communication, field teams, customer communication, or operational connectivity are relevant.

Campaign configuration already supports secondary product positioning, including Elula Mobile.

## Commercial Operating Model

The Prospect Engine should support centralized operations:

- one controlled source of processed prospect data;
- clear campaign structure;
- controlled Elula BizHub sync;
- duplicate prevention;
- owner assignment;
- appointment-focused reporting.

## Success Metrics

Primary metric:

- qualified appointments generated.

Supporting metrics:

- prospects processed;
- prospects imported;
- duplicates skipped;
- opportunities created;
- tasks created;
- appointments booked;
- conversion from imported prospect to appointment;
- campaign source quality.
